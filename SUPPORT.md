# Chell Support

If you need help with Chell, use one of the options below. These channels are actively monitored by the team.

## Quick help
- Join our community Discord for real-time questions and onboarding: https://discord.gg/2gwYUwve58

## Bug reports or feature requests
- Open a GitHub issue so we can track and prioritize your request: https://github.com/Cerulin/Chell/issues
  - Include your platform, Chell version, steps to reproduce (if applicable), and any logs or screenshots that help us investigate.

## Direct inquiries
- Email the team at contact@cerulin.com for account questions or anything you prefer to keep private.

## Response times
- Discord: typically within a few hours during the workweek
- GitHub issues: triaged within 1–2 business days
- Email: responses within 1–2 business days
